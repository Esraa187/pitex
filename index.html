Our Process: From Harvest to Bottle

We combine traditional Mediterranean expertise with German-engineered stainless steel precision. Our multi-stage extraction process ensures the lowest possible acidity and the highest concentration of polyphenols.

  1. 01

    Receiving & Washing

    Immediately upon harvest, olives enter our high-pressure stainless steel washing system. Precision filtration removes any residual soil or leaves, ensuring only the purest fruit proceeds to extraction.

  2. 02

    Hammer Crushing

    Our high-torque industrial crushers process whole olives into a fine paste. The mechanics are designed to minimize heat generation, protecting the volatile aromatic compounds from early oxidation.

  3. 03

    Cold Malaxation

    The paste is slowly mixed in temperature-controlled malaxer tanks. At precisely 27°C, enzyme activity releases oil droplets from the pulp cells, preparing for the centrifugal separation phase.

  4. 04

    Centrifugal Decanter

    The core of our line: a high-speed three-phase decanter separates oil from water and pomace. This closed-system extraction occurs in seconds, maintaining total anaerobic conditions for maximum freshness.

  5. 05

    Automated Bottling

    Final filtration leads directly to our automatic bottling line. Each UV-protective glass bottle is vacuum-sealed and nitrogen-flushed to preserve the liquid gold for up to 24 months of shelf life.
